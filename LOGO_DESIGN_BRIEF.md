### Icon Ideas

1. **Layered Sphere** (Recommended)
   - 3-4 concentric arcs forming a sphere
   - Represents full-stack layers
   - Modern, clean, professional

2. **Code Sphere**
   - Code brackets `{ }` or `<>` forming sphere shape
   - Tech-forward, developer-friendly
   - Memorable and unique

3. **Stacked Layers**
   - Horizontal bars stacked in sphere formation
   - Visual representation of "stack"
   - Simple, scalable
